How to export GPX from popular platforms

Garmin Connect Direct import coming soon

  1. Open your course/activity on connect.garmin.com
  2. Click the gear icon (top right) and select "Export to GPX"
  3. Upload the downloaded .gpx file above

Komoot Direct import coming soon

  1. Open your tour on komoot.com
  2. Click "More" then "Download GPX"
  3. Upload the downloaded .gpx file above

Strava

  1. Open the route on strava.com
  2. Click the "..." menu and select "Export GPX"
  3. Upload the downloaded .gpx file above

RideWithGPS

  1. Open the route on ridewithgps.com
  2. Click "Export" and choose GPX Track
  3. Upload the file, or copy the direct GPX URL and paste above
